Muppets Rescued! The Alarming Muppet Kidnapping

Kermit Frog and Piggy Lee were kidnapped. With the most unlikely kidnapper to be held accountable, Not-So-Local News Today has all the details. Kermit's kidnapper reportedly wouldn't stop talking about needing another 50¢ before being able to get what it needed. What monster needs money, one might ask? The answer is simple: The Loch Ness Monster. Kermit was able to draw this from memory in an interview just after his escape.

Eye witness accounts

It would appear as though the Loch Ness Monster is once again on a monetary-themed rampage, combing through the world in search of just enough to procure what it REALLY wants. But, what on Earth DOES it really want? What would any monster want to buy for $3.50?

Kermit had this to say: While finally making our escape from our captor, we saw it's vending machine. I just don't understand why something that big would want a salad. And so badly that it would kidnap and steal for it! Diets are nice, but c'mon! It doesn't even open those things, it just eats it whole! Glass and all! I guess this is just what we get for wanting a nice quiet walk by the lake.

Just days after gaining her freedom, Piggy Miss Piggy Lee has a new perspective on life: You take things for granted, when you're a star. But, when you're SOMEONE'S star, it becomes abundantly clear that you probably won't get to be who and what you want anymore. There's no more holding back Missy Piggy! I almost lost my shot at being the biggest actress, singer, dancer, musician ... ahem... et cetera! No Loch Ness Monster is gonna be taking that away from ME! I am gonna have it all!

Not letting it hold them back

Now that they're out of the clutches of what may have been an evil monster or a deranged creature needing help, these two are back in the limelight. It's happiness abound for the moment, but just how long will it last? Will Nessy find them again to collect what it feels it's owed? After all, Kermit tells us how important a diet can be to...will Nessy agree?

Either way, these muppets are back to living the dream, though Kermit can be seen still wary
